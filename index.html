ABOUT ME

I'm a self-motivated, aspiring web developer with a thirst for knowledge. Keen interest in JavaScript, but am no stranger to learning new programming languages or skills on demand. I take pride in my ability to produce top quality work while continuing to find ways to better myself daily.

I have experience in JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS3 and Sass. I’m a recent graduate from New York Code and Design’s Full Stack web intensive program. Prior to pursuing programming, I studied e-Commerce at Philadelphia University eventually developing an interest in front end development and visual aesthetics. Career focus towards Front End Web Development, Marketing Management, and General Business.
